🌿Greenleaf Cosmetics 🌿

Natural, handmade and sustainable cosmetics.

Lothh Greenleaf is the online alter ego of María Polonio, the brains, heart and hands of Greenleaf Cosmetics. She became first interested in natural cosmetics when her mother's aloe plants helped her manage and eventually get rid of a particularly nasty dermatitis flare up. She started learning about natural formulation thanks to Victoria Moradell's online course and became fascinated with the creativity and potential of botanicals and other natural elements. She continues learning and practising and is very excited to become a Formula Botanica student.
